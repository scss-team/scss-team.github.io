What we do

Welcome to the Student Multimedia Agency! Whether you're just starting in your program or a year in, our site will help you gain some great connections with other students in different programs, Algonquin College Faculty or catch the eye of potential future employers. On the main page, you will see a slider of some featured work from different students, our featured program of the month where you see selected students work and a link to their products and or services. Each student chooses what they would like to put on the site whether that is design services to web page products. We currently only have 3 programs involved at the moment more will be added as interest and requests increase! :) Interested in connecting with a particular student? No problem! On the contact page there is a dropdown of all students, categorized by the program they are in, simply select their name fill out the form of your information and that's it! Easy Peezy!
